Literature

Literature is the art of sharing stories through written words such as books, poems, essays and more. The earliest forms of literature such as law codes date back all the way to ancient Mesopotamia. Since then, there have been four distinct periods of literature – Romanticism, Realism, Naturalism, and Modernism.





Painting

Painting is one of the few foundations of art. It all began with a cave and a caveman. Important movements in painting include the Renaissance, Impressionism, Pop Art and Surrealism. There are different kinds of mediums within the painting realm such as watercolor, acrylic and oil paint.





Graphic Design

Graphic design is the overlapping between art and technology such as the advertisements we see all around us today. This form of art is digital and the goal is to create designs such as typography or digital illustrations. This use of technology is utilized to tell a story.





Illustration

Illustration is an art style that interprets text into a visual story. While graphic design focuses on digital storytelling,illustrations center around the interpretation of text. With the rise of technology, this medium has become widely popular. Many use their Adobe Creative Cloud or Procreate for this execution.









Sculpture

Sculpture dates back to before the first cave painting. It is the making of two or three-dimensional forms. This is executed by carving into a material. Greek sculpture is the most recognizable era of sculpture because artists began sculpting realistic human figures.





Film

Film is a style of art that depicts moving pictures on a screen which tells a story. Since the beginnings of film, the industry has boomed and become bigger than ever. But the art of film doesn’t just mean movies, it also means short films, sketches, or even YouTube videos and Tik Toks.





Music

Music is instrumentals that create a harmonious sound together. Popular genres include pop, indie, folk, rap, punk, and much more music. To create music doesn’ mean paying for an expensive studio and recording a song, but rather a pen rhythmically hitting the table or a squeak being made by the laundry machine.





Architecture

Without even thinking about it, architecture is all around us. From house to restaurants to school, there is the use of architecture. It is the art of designing and constructing buildings. While many homes are built for function, the elements of design are still considered art.





Theater

Simply put, theater is the act of acting, writing play, or directing. This combination of acting, movement and singing represents a physical representation of a story, whether fact or fiction. With the recent pandemic, there have been new innovations created such as live streaming shows.





Fashion

Fashion is the art of styling of clothes to make a statement. Besides clothing, accessories, makeup and hairstyles are also aparat of the fashion industry. While many pieces in a fashion show are usually created with an artistic purpose rather than functionality purpose, street style has blown up in the past years.
